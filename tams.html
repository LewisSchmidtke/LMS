I have since joined BMW M Motorsport as a data/performance engineering intern. In this role I worked on competitor analysis and performance tracking, thus halting the active development of TAMS.

Overview

DATA WINS RACES

Modern race cars generate millions of data points per session across hundreds of sensors. TAMS transforms this data into actionable insights for race engineers and strategists. Leveraging historical data to analyze trends and analyzing session-to-session developments for maximum performance.

Data Source: Alkamel Systems (WEC & IMSA) | OpenF1 API (Formula 1)
